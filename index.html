GradeX Peer Assessment

GradeX is an online intuitive tool that empowers students to peer assess themselves, facilitated by their teachers. Research has shown that Peer Assessment is an efficient way to create a student-centered learning environment, build trust, accountability, and objective critique skills in students. These skillsets have been found to play significant roles in students' achievement even after formative school years.
